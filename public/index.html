5 signs you could be getting more from your IT data relationship
16 February 2016

These days the term “Big Data” is one of the buzziest of the buzzwords out there, reaching such a level that even people outside of IT and data analytics have become desensetized to it. I find this truly unfortunate, especially with regard to those non-IT folks. The reason is that when approached properly, Big Data is capable of some pretty amazing things totally unrelated to technology and number crunching.
